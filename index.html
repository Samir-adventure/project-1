COVID-19

Lakeside Center is actively working to create a clean, safe, professional environment during Covid-19. Special measures include:

  • Nightly janitorial disinfecting all touch surfaces
  • Day Porter sanitizing, routinely disinfecting all touch surfaces
  • Nightly HVAC air purge, and system run 24/7 for maximum fresh air replacement
  • MERV13 air filtration for conditioned air
  • Touchless hand sanitizers in common areas
  • Touchless water bottle dispensers
  • Touchless restroom fixtures
  • Touchless conference room reservations
